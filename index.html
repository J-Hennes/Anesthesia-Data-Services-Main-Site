Our Mission

Anesthesia Data Services, LLC (ADS) is focused on helping anesthesia practices and surgical facilities capture, process, and share operational data. Our goal is to create the data management tools needed to execute on complex contractual relationships such as revenue guarantee and locums based models. We are also building tools to enhance communication between anesthesia groups and their surgical facility partners.

What We Can Do

Our software allows providers to capture daily work information and calculates provider payment and facility invoicing based on defined contracts. ADS also allows surgical facilities to submit coverage requests and daily schedules, while anesthesia groups can use the platform to create schedules, assign providers, and collect provider's daily work information.
